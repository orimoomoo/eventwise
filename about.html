About Eventwise

At Eventwise, we believe planning family events should be fun, not frantic! Whether it’s a cozy birthday party, a summer BBQ, or a big holiday reunion, EventPlanner is your go-to sidekick for organizing the little details—and the big moments—without the hassle. Create guest lists in a snap, track who’s bringing what (Aunt Linda’s famous potato salad, anyone?), set handy reminders so you never forget the cake, and even split costs effortlessly. With easy-to-use tools and friendly nudges, you’ll spend less time stressing and more time making memories. Because the best family gatherings aren’t just planned… they’re enjoyed!

Why choose Eventwise?

✔ Stress-Free Planning – Ditch the chaos! Our all-in-one tool handles invites, budgets, and schedules so you can enjoy the fun.

✔ Made for Families, by Families – Designed with real-life needs in mind—think potlucks, birthday surprises, and last-minute changes (because kids happen).

✔ Smart Guest Management – Track RSVPs, who’s bringing what (Uncle Joe’s famous chili, anyone?), and send reminders in one place.

✔ Budget Like a Pro – Set spending limits, split costs, and avoid surprises with real-time expense tracking.

✔ Customizable Checklists – From decorations to grocery runs, never forget a task with step-by-step guides tailored to your event.

✔ Shared Access – Collaborate effortlessly—share plans with spouses, grandparents, or the entire PTA committee.

✔ Offline Friendly – No Wi-Fi? No problem! Access your plans anytime, anywhere.

✔ No Tech Skills Needed – Super intuitive, with a little dash of fun (emojis included! 😊).

Because life’s best moments happen off-screen—we’ll handle the rest!

Our story

Born from Chaos, Built for Joy We started EventPlanner after one disastrous family reunion—where the cake was forgotten, half the guests showed up late, and Uncle Dave somehow lost the BBQ directions

Frustrated by clunky tools and endless spreadsheets, we asked: Why can’t planning an event feel as joyful as the event itself?

So in 2025, we built EventPlanner—a tool that’

Simple enough for last-minute birthday parties.

Powerful enough for holiday feasts with 50+ relatives.

Human enough to remember that life happens (like when your toddler “helps” by deleting your guest list).

Today, we’re a small team of parents, foodies, and memory-makers, obsessed with turning stress into celebration—one RSVP, checklist, and (well-tracked) potluck dish at a time.

Because the best gatherings aren’t just planned… they’re lived.
