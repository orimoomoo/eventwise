About Eventwise

At Eventwise, we believe every event—whether an intimate birthday or a grand corporate gala—should be as unique as the people celebrating it.

Founded in 2025 by Team Nala, we blend meticulous planning, creative flair, and cutting-edge tech to turn your ideas into unforgettable experiences.

Why Choose Us?

  • ✅ Stress-Free Planning: Our app and team handle the logistics—you enjoy the magic.
  • ✅ Tailored Creativity: No cookie-cutter events. We design around your story.
  • ✅ Tech Meets Touch: AI-powered tools and human experts for seamless execution.
  • 💥 100% You-Focused: From budget hacks to VIP vendor access, we’ve got your back.

Our Story

What started as a humble beginning grew into 500+ events planned islandwide — all because we’re obsessed with making your milestones effortless and extraordinary.
